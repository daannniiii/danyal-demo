About Carnival Corner

Your trusted partner in event management and ticket reservations

Who We Are

Carnival Corner is Pakistan's premier event management and ticket reservation platform, connecting event organizers, vendors, and attendees across major cities including Lahore, Karachi, and Islamabad.

We believe in creating unforgettable experiences by simplifying the event discovery and booking process. Our platform offers a seamless way to find, book, and attend the events you love, all while providing event organizers with powerful tools to manage their events efficiently.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the event industry in Pakistan by providing a comprehensive, user-friendly platform that brings people together through amazing experiences.

What We Offer

  • Easy event discovery based on your city and area
  • Interactive seat selection for a personalized booking experience
  • Secure ticket reservations
  • Vendor registration for event stalls and kiosks
  • Comprehensive admin tools for event managers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book tickets?
Simply browse events in your city, select your preferred event, choose your seats using our interactive seat map, and complete the booking form with your details.
Can I select specific seats?
Yes! Our interactive seat map allows you to visually select your preferred seats. Available seats are shown in green, selected in yellow, and booked seats in red.
How do I register as a vendor?
Visit our Vendor Registration page, fill out the form with your business details, select the event you're interested in, and submit. Our team will review and contact you within 2-3 business days.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile wallet payments for your convenience.
Can I cancel my booking?
Yes, you can cancel your booking up to 48 hours before the event for a full refund. Please contact our support team for assistance.
How do I become an event organizer?
If you're interested in hosting events on our platform, please contact our admin team at admin@carnivalcorner.pk for partnership opportunities.
Are there any hidden fees?
No! The price you see is the price you pay. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden charges.
How do I receive my tickets?
After booking confirmation, you'll receive an email with your e-ticket and booking details. Simply show this at the event entrance.
